About us

The 51st edition of the premier annual Inter IIT Sports Meet (Inter IIT, as students commonly call it) will be hosted by IIT Kanpur this year. The meet shall comprise of an Inter IIT Aquatics Meet from October 2-October 5, 2016 ,the Main Inter IIT Sports Meet from December 12-December 19, 2016 followed by Staff Meet from *-----*. This comes after the 51st Inter IIT scheduled in December,2015 in IIT Madras was cancelled due to incessant rains in Chennai. This is for the ninth time that IIT Kanpur has been entrusted with the responsibility of hosting this prestigious tournament.
